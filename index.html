BMW lineup

The Bavarian company BMW produces passenger cars and sports cars for various market segments. The BMW X5 and X6 SUVs, executive sedans of the seventh series are gaining more and more popularity in Russia. The BMW model range is also represented by business class cars (BMW 5 series), sports coupes (BMW M8), convertibles and roadsters (BMW 8 series and Z4). The list is supplemented by small and medium class cars in the body of a sedan or station wagon (BMW 3 series) and compact hatchbacks (BMW 1 series hatchback).
Cars today
Today, the company produces very high-quality cars that are popular not only at home, but throughout the world. Today, the car of this concern has become a symbol of German quality and reliability.
